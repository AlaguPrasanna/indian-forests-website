Forests in India

A forest is a living thing; each plant, each tree, each insect, each animal has its role to play. Together they create a complex and delicate ecosystem.The forest is not a resource for us, it is life itself. It is the only place for us to live.

Evergreen forest


The forest receives heavy rainfall and sheds its leaves throughout the years.They are also useful in the continual survival of animals and plants in the forest ecosystem. and more..

Deciduous forest


They are found in central India with broad leaves and shed their leaves only in one season throughout the years.A long dry season followed by heavy rainfall season and more..

Thorn forest


They are found in dry areas of western, central, and southern India.The tropical thorn forests in India are found in the areas of southwest Punjab, in Haryana, Rajasthan,and Uttar Pradesh and more..

Coniferous forest


The 'Eastern Himalayan subalpine conifer forests is a temperate coniferous forests ecoregion which is found in the middle and upper elevations of the eastern Middle Himalayas, in western Nepal and Bhutan and more..
